Our history

Built around the entrepreneur.

Acuity was founded with the central goal of turning entrepreneurs into better business owners. We have worked since day 1 to keep more money in our clients' pockets. While we have grown as a firm, our commitment to small business owners in our communities has never changed.

Our passion

Local advisors. Statewide reach.

With offices in multiple locations and a state of the art cloud based platform, we are capable of servicing clients anywhere they may be. As entrepreneurs ourselves, we believe in serving the business owners of our communities with the best possible tax plan, actionable financial analysis, and timely results. We are dedicated to proactive planning for your business now and into the future.
