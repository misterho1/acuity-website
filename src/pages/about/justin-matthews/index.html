Justin Matthews

Tax & Business Advisor · Director, Client Services

justin@expectacuity.com Schedule with Justin

Justin Matthews loves helping entrepreneurs across the country become more successful. He has extensive background in the tax and accounting industry and is a well-known thought-leader helping small business owners increase their profit margins through strategic planning and execution.

Prior to joining Acuity Business & Tax Advisors, Justin ran his own tax and accounting business for five years. During this time, Justin also was a lead consultant and presenter for Thomson Reuters where he helped coach thousands of other tax professionals on how to give better proactive tax and accounting services to their clients. Justin started his tax and accounting career working with two large regional CPA firms specializing in taxation of partnerships, s-corporations, and individuals.

Justin has served in several leadership positions in his community, helping others achieve their goals and reach their highest potential. He holds a Bachelor of Science degree in accounting from Dixie State University, and a Master of Accountancy degree from Southern Utah University.

In his free time, Justin enjoys hunting and camping in the mountains of Southern Utah with his family.
