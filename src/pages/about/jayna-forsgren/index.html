Jayna Forsgren

Tax & Business Advisor · Director, Client Services

jayna@expectacuity.com Schedule with Jayna

Jayna is a graduate of Weber State University with a bachelor degree in Accounting and a Master of Professional Accountancy in Tax.

Jayna is dedicated to the profession and has over 15 years of experience in public accounting. She is the current recipient of the Utah Association of Certified Public Accountants (UACPA) 2019 Experience Leader: Women to Watch.

Jayna is also a wife and mother to three very active children and makes a point to attend as many of their activities as she can. She serves on the Board of the Ogden Weber Community Action Partnership and is an active member of the UACPA and Women in Business.
