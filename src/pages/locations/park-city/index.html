Where we add the most value in Park City

The patterns we see most.

  • Short-term rental and hospitality entity structuring

    Material participation tests, vacation-home loss limitations, and the entity choice that matches how the property is actually operated.

  • Multi-state residency planning

    For owners who split time between Utah and another state, the residency determination has direct tax consequences worth getting right before year-end.

  • Concentrated equity events

    Park City attracts executives with stock-comp tax exposure. We model AMT consequences and exercise timing across multiple years.

  • Real-estate-driven tax planning

    1031 exchanges, cost segregation on commercial holdings, and coordination with broader investment portfolios.
