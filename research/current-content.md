4. **Robert East has no bio and no headshot** ("Coming soon" / "Photo Coming Soon" placeholder). Has been like this since the 2019-era launch.
5. **Jayna Forsgren has no headshot** ("Photo Coming Soon" placeholder).

9. **No service-specific landing pages.** Services are bulleted lists with no individual descriptions, no per-service pages, and no SEO-friendly long-form content.
10. **No social-media links** anywhere on the site (no Facebook/LinkedIn/Instagram).
11. **No privacy policy or terms of service** linked from the footer.
12. **Copyright is stale** (2019) on every page.
13. **Typo preserved:** Jayna Forsgren's bio says "Ogden Weber Communicty Action Partnership" — should be "Community". Decide whether to silently fix on rebuild.
14. **Brand identity ambiguity:** Logo alt text says "EAST FOLSOM LLC" (apparently the legal entity), but the public-facing brand is "Acuity Business & Tax Advisors, LLC". Confirm which name to lead with on the rebuild.
15. **Image alt text is generic** (mostly "Picture" or empty). All images need real alt text for accessibility on the rebuild.
16. **Meta descriptions are sparse.** Only Home and What we do have OG descriptions; the others are missing or are just "Copyright (c) 2019".
17. **Photo style on About is inconsistent** — three directors have brick-wall headshots; Jayna does not.
18. **Phone number duplication:** Ogden and St. George share `(385) 333-4050`, while South Ogden has `(801) 393-7884`. Confirm this is intentional / current.
