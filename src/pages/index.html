Common questions

Before your first session.

What does a free Strategy Session cover?

A 30-minute conversation about your current tax and accounting situation. We listen first, identify two or three opportunities or risks specific to your business, and explain what an ongoing relationship would look like. There is no pitch. If we are not the right fit, we will tell you.

Do you only work with Utah businesses?

Most of our clients are based in Utah, but we serve clients in many states from our cloud-based platform. If your business is multi-state or your personal return crosses jurisdictions, we are well set up to handle it — Brad, Justin, and Jayna all have multi-state experience.

What is the difference between a tax advisor and a tax preparer?

A tax preparer files your return based on what already happened. A tax advisor works with you all year — before transactions occur — to position your business and your personal finances so the return reflects a deliberate strategy. Filing is the last step, not the only step.

How are fees structured?

Most clients are on a flat monthly fee that bundles tax planning, return preparation, and ongoing communication (calls, meetings, software help) so there is no hourly meter running when you have a question. We will quote a specific fee after the Strategy Session.

What software do you support?

We work with most modern small-business stacks. Bring whatever you have — QuickBooks Online, Xero, spreadsheets, or paper — and we will help you get to a clean, reliable system you can run from. Specific platform partnerships listed on this site once confirmed.
