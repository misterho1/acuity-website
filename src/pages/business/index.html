What we do for businesses

Two ways we work together.

Most engagements start with strategy and move into ongoing maintenance. You can pick one or both.

01 · Advisory

Tax & Business Advisory

Our first goal is to identify your goals and pain points. Then, we teach you about tax savings and accounting best practices. We work all of these into a strategy unique to you and your business. This strategy will guide all decisions we make together. We keep it updated with our approach to planning that includes keeping our eye on tax changes and relevant industry data. We are always looking for ways to improve your plan and stand ready to make adjustments as your goals and pain points change.

See advisory services
02 · Maintenance

Tax & Accounting Maintenance

Once we have helped you implement your tax and accounting strategies, we go into proactive maintenance mode. We work with you to identify an ongoing relationship that ensures you have the right tools and resources at your fingertips to run a healthy business and make educated financial decisions. We continually monitor and find all the changes that affect you and help you prepare yourself and your business. Because our maintenance plan starts with your goals, they form the basis of the ongoing tax and accounting services you need.

How it works

Three steps. No surprises.

01

Strategy session

A 30-minute conversation about your business, your goals, and where the tax code is helping or hurting you. We listen first.

02

Plan

We write a strategy unique to you — entity structure, income mix, retirement, fringe benefits, multi-year tax projection. The plan is the document we work from.

03

Maintain

Ongoing accounting, payroll, projections, and quarterly check-ins. When the tax code or your business changes, the plan changes with it.
